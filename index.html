About Us

Binari Intelligence (BI) was founded by Roshini Kesavan.  She has a wealth of experience from her tenure at Microsoft (USA) and a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from Georgia Institute of Technology (USA).  At BI, our mission is to provide top-notch software solutions to businesses of all sizes.  We work collaboratively with our clients to custom tailor a solution that caters to their unique need.  Our team comprises of experienced SMEs, Architects, Project Managers, Designers and Software Developers adept with the latest technologies and tools.  BI strives to exceed customer expectations by providing cutting-edge software solutions and services.
