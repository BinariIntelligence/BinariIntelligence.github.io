About Us

Binari Intelligence was established under the leadership of Roshini Kesavan, a Software Developer with a distinguished six-year tenure at Microsoft and a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from the prestigious Georgia Institute of Technology. At Binari Intelligence (BI), our mission is to provide top-notch software solutions to businesses of all sizes. Our team comprises of experienced Project Managers, Designers and Software Developers, each bringing a wealth of experience to the table. Leveraging the latest technologies and tools we ensure our clients benefit from cutting-edge solutions that meet the highest industry standards. Central to our approach is a collaborative engagement with clients. We work closely to comprehend their unique requirements and tailor our solutions accordingly. Binari Intelligence stands as a beacon of professional excellence, dedicated to providing unparalleled software solutions and surpassing expectations.
